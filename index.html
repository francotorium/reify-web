HOW IT WORKS

Boutique Partners

Pick your favorite clothes from one of our partners to get them altered to get that perfect fit. There they will take your body measurements and the alterations you want.

Get It Altered

Once the clothes are ordered, we ship them to our Reify seamstress who will tailor the clothes to your measurements while you can see the progress of your order with your Reify closet dashboard

Ship it to You

After our seamstresses alter your clothes, they are sent straight to you! No charges are made until we confirm your items are altered correctly.
